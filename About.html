Using bright colors, hard work and experience, Rachel Roy loves to create little pieces that reflect who she is. She is passionate about the little things; like the gray hairs of a dog or the bright colors of a house that is decorated in holiday lights.

She also is a photographer that takes pictures of what she sees in the world, nature, animals, and friendships. If requested, she can help take pictures for prom, yearbook photos, wedding photos, and more.

Rachel is also a big fan of jewelry making and loves Christmas, she takes her love of both to create different pieces that she sells and keeps. She also takes a portion of her earnings and she donates it to those in need.

Rachel takes a lot of her inspiration from her pet dog, Milo, and many other dogs that she takes care of. As well as an artist she also pet sits so she has a lot of time with animals, mainly dogs.
